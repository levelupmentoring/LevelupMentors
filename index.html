ORGANISATION AIMS




Volunteer

Our team commit time to LevelUp aims on a voluntary basis. This way we can ensure that our team are truly dedicated to the cause.



Develop

Our development programmes are designed to promote employability skills, confidence and resilience in young people.



Mentor

Our mentoring scheme supports pupils through major life transitions to ensure their continual progression.





COVID-19 Response

The current economic crisis risks could increase youth unemployment by 600,000 this year. The issue of non-continuation in the UK before the outbreak is likely to be exacerbated. This is why we have teamed up with make_sense.org and the Laidlaw Foundation to take concrete action in the face of the coronavirus epidemic.
