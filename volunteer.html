Volunteer with us

What are the volunteering opportunites?
A wide variety of voluntary opportunites are available when working with LevelUp. You could volunteer your time to support:
  • Youth Mentoring
  • Skills Workshops
  • Business Support
  • Admin
  • Networking
  • Social Media
  • Advice/Support
  • And More...

    • How volunteering can help you?
    At LevelUp we believe that mentoring should be reciprocal. We want to support our volunteers as much as possible in their skill development, confidence building, and employability.
    Working with LevelUp has many benefits for our volunteers. You will be welcomed into a dedicated team where you can learn new skills and develop a sense of belonging within your community whilst helping to improve the quality of life for young people.

    Volunteering gives you the chance to:
  • Develop your CV
  • Meet new people
  • Contribute to your community
  • Pursue meaningful work
  • Undertake training
